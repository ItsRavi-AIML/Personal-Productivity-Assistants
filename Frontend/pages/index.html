Process

From upload
to insight in four steps.

01

Upload your documents

Drag in PDFs, text files, meeting transcripts, or notes. NEXUS accepts any unstructured data you throw at it.

02

Automatic vectorization

Documents are chunked and converted into vector embeddings, then stored in your private vector database — ready for semantic search.

03

Ask in plain language

Type any question — "What did we decide in last Tuesday's meeting?" or "Summarize Chapter 4 of my notes." NEXUS retrieves the most relevant context.

04

Get accurate, grounded answers

Responses are generated with RAG — anchored to your actual documents, not generic knowledge. Zero hallucinations about your private data.
